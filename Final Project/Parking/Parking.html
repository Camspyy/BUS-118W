Parking Information

General Parking will be between major avenues that border Central Park. These areas will be patroled by security teams during the hours of the event. Please do not park in unofficial lots.

Accesible Parking will be provided near the festival entrance.

Taxi Drop Offs will have a designated area which will be open during the hours of the event.

The following activities are prohibited in the parking lot:
- Tailgating Loitering, Drinking Alcohol, Illegal drug use, Camping/Sleeping in your vehicle
